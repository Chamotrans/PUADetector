🔒 PUA Detector — Privacy Policy / 私隱政策

Last updated / 最後更新：May 28, 2026

EN Privacy First

PUA Detector is designed to be completely private. All audio processing and speech recognition happens entirely on your device. Nothing is ever sent to the cloud, and no data leaves your phone.

中 私隱優先

PUA Detector 的設計以私隱為核心。所有音頻處理和語音識別均在您的裝置上完成，不需連接雲端，您的資料絕不會離開手機。

EN Data We Collect

We collect no data. PUA Detector does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or crash reports. There are no servers, no accounts, no tracking.

The app uses Apple's built-in SFSpeechRecognizer framework, which performs speech recognition on-device. No audio recordings or transcripts are ever sent to Apple or any third party.

中 我們收集的資料

我們不收集任何資料。PUA Detector 不會收集、儲存、傳輸或分享任何個人資料、使用數據或崩潰報告。沒有伺服器、沒有帳戶、沒有追蹤。

應用程式使用 Apple 內置的 SFSpeechRecognizer 框架，所有語音識別均在裝置上完成，不會將任何音頻或文字傳送給 Apple 或第三方。

EN Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device via UserDefaults and can be cleared at any time in the app's settings:

  • Detection sensitivity preference
  • Alert sound preference
  • Classifier category toggles
  • Calibration feedback data (used to improve local detection only)

中 本地儲存的資料

以下資料僅儲存在您的裝置上（透過 UserDefaults），可隨時在 App 設定中清除：

  • 偵測敏感度偏好
  • 警報聲音偏好
  • 分類器類別開關
  • 校準反饋數據（僅用於改善本地偵測）

EN Third-Party Services

PUA Detector uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The app has zero network dependencies — no internet connection is required for core functionality.

中 第三方服務

PUA Detector 不使用任何第三方分析、廣告或追蹤 SDK。應用程式無需網絡連線即可使用核心功能。

EN Microphone & Speech Recognition

The app requires microphone and speech recognition permissions solely for the purpose of real-time PUA detection. These permissions are requested on first launch via Apple's standard permission dialog. You can revoke them at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security.

The app may listen in the background when you explicitly start detection and enable background mode. Background listening is clearly indicated by a visual indicator in the app.

中 麥克風與語音識別

App 需要麥克風和語音識別權限，僅用於實時 PUA 偵測。首次啟動時會透過 Apple 標準權限對話框請求權限，您可隨時在「設定 → 私隱與安全」中撤回權限。

當您明確開始偵測並啟用背景模式時，App 可能在背景繼續聆聽，App 內會有視覺指示器清楚標示。

中 政策變更

本私隱政策的任何變更將在此頁面公布。變更後繼續使用 App 即表示您接受更新後的政策。
