EN Frequently Asked Questions

🔹 How does PUA Detector work?

The app uses Apple's on-device speech recognition to listen to conversations and analyze speech patterns in real time. When it detects language associated with psychological manipulation, it alerts you.

App 使用 Apple 的本地語音識別功能，實時分析對話中的言語模式。當偵測到與心理操控相關的語言時，會發出警報。

🔹 Is my audio sent to the cloud?

No. Everything runs on your device. No audio, transcripts, or any data ever leaves your phone.

不會。一切在您的裝置上運行，不會有任何音頻、文字或數據離開您的手機。

🔹 Can I use it in the background?

Yes. Start detection and enable background mode. The app will continue listening even when your phone is locked. A visual indicator shows when the app is actively listening.

可以。開始偵測並啟用背景模式後，即使手機鎖定，App 仍會繼續聆聽。App 內會有視覺指示器顯示正在聆聽。

🔹 How accurate is the detection?

PUA Detector is a safety awareness tool, not a diagnostic device. Detection is based on linguistic patterns and may not always be accurate. Please use your own judgment.

PUA Detector 是一個安全意識工具，並非診斷裝置。偵測基於語言模式，未必完全準確。請務必運用自己的判斷力。

🔹 What languages are supported?

Cantonese (Hong Kong), Mandarin (Taiwan/China), and English. The classifier includes PUA phrases specific to each language and cultural context.

支援粵語（香港）、普通話（台灣/中國）及英語。分類器包含每種語言和文化背景相關的 PUA 語句。

🔹 Can I export my detection reports?

Yes. Detection reports can be shared via the iOS share sheet as text. You can save, print, or send them to any app.

可以。偵測報告可透過 iOS 分享列表以文字形式匯出，您可以儲存、列印或傳送至任何應用程式。
